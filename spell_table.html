Spell Assemble Table

The spells assemble table has three uses.

  1. Change the spells on a wand
  2. Upgrade a wand
  3. Craft custom items

Wand Upgrade Menu:

Tier Upgrade Magicial Material Vanilla Material
Tier 2 Upgrade Magicial Wood 10 Gold Ingots
Tier 3 Upgrade Purified Wand Core 3 Gold Blocks
Tier 4 Upgrade Obsidian Shaft 10 Iron Blocks
Tier 5 Upgrade Netherite Core 32 Gold Blocks
